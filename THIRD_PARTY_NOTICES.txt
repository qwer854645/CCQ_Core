Third-Party Gun Packs — ccq_core
=================================

CCQ Default Gun Pack (ccq_default_gun)
--------------------------------------

  Embedded in ccq_core and installed to .minecraft/tacz/ccq_default_gun when missing.
  Derived from TACZ Default Gun Pack (TACZ Dev Team, CC BY-NC-ND 4.0).

Applied Armorer (TaCZ gun pack)
-------------------------------

  Applied Armorer itself is NOT bundled in ccq_core.
  The modpack must provide the official gun pack under .minecraft/tacz/.

  When an Applied Armorer pack is already present, ccq_core may apply a CCQ patch:

    File:  data/applied_armorer/data/blocks/worckbench_applied_armorer_data.json
    Patch: patches/applied_armorer/worckbench_applied_armorer_data.json

  Purpose: restore gun terminal tabs on TaCZ 1.21.1 for Create Craft & Quiet.

  This patch is CCQ configuration, not part of Applied Armorer.
  Applied Armorer remains CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 (Koei, Ithaqua).
  https://space.bilibili.com/407541278

When sharing ccq_core, retain this NOTICE.
